Indian Dance Heritage

Nritya AI celebrates the diversity of Indian dance forms, from classical Kathak and Bharatanatyam to Bollywood and contemporary styles.
Our platform aims to preserve, teach, and innovate using technology, making dance accessible and interactive for everyone.

  • Bollywood: Vibrant, expressive, and energetic
  • Kathak: Graceful spins and intricate footwork
  • Hip-Hop: Urban, freestyle, and powerful
  • Contemporary: Fluid, creative, and modern

Our Mission

To democratize dance education and creativity using AI.
To support solo practice, group choreography, and teaching with real-time feedback and intelligent tools.
